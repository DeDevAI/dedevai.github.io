DeDev.AI

Democratize AI Development

Vision

  • Within the next decade, the vast majority of software and programs will be written by AI, or so called 'AI developers'.
  • Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) can't be achieved without those `AI developers` who modify their own codebase and self-evolve themselves.
  • We believe not only building 'AI developers' is important, but they ought to be open, cost-effective, and universally accessible to all individuals.

Mission

  • DeDev.AI's mission is to aggregate the collective computational power of commodity GPUs from around the globe, and dedicate them to creating an open, powerful and affordable 'AI developer' owned and driven by the community.
